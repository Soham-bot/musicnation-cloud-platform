How audio streaming works

1. You click play on a track

2. Frontend calls /stream/<id> on the Flask backend

3. Flask generates a pre-signed S3 URL (valid 1 hour)

4. Your browser streams the MP3 directly from S3 — no data passes through Flask

5. Files never become public — each URL is unique and time-limited
